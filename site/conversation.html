If you disagree seek agreement. Be prepared to change you mind. You might misunderstand or be ignorant. Disagreement can be for teaching but also for being taught.

    Sine qua non

    1. Acknowledge the emotions you bring into a dispute, or those that arise.
    2. Make your own assumptions explicit.
    3. Attempt impartiality.
